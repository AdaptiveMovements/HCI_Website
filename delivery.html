Delivery Options

Delivery detail for items despatched directly from the manufacturer can be found on the product listing.

Unfortunately, at this time we cannot deliver to the Channel Islands, Isle of Man or British Forces posted overseas.

PLEASE NOTE Our carrier deliveries are made between the hours of 7am and 7pm.

UK Standard:

Within 3-5 working days of your order being placed - £2.99 or FREE delivery on orders over £100
- Applies to George and selected ASDA Direct Small/Medium products.

UK Next Day :

Order before 9pm weekdays and 2pm Sundays to receive the following day – £4.99 or FREE delivery on orders over £150.
- Applies to George and selected ASDA Direct Small/Medium products.

Order before 2pm on Saturday to receive the following day.
- Applies to George and selected ASDA Direct Small/Medium products.

UK Next Day Evening:

Order until midnight for delivery the next day between 6pm and 10pm - £9.99. Place your order between midnight and 1am for same day delivery between 6pm and 10pm.

- Applies to George and selected ASDA Direct Small/Medium products.
